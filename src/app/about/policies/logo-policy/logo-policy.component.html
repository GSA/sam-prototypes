Graphic Standards

The SAM.gov logo is intended to be displayed as provided in the downloadable files on this web page. The list of prohibited changes includes, but is not limited to, the following.

  • Do not change the aspect ratio of the logo, stretching it or skewing it in any way.
  • Do not change the relative size of the work mark and the graphic symbol.
  • Do not display the logo against an image, patterned, or multi-colored background. The preferred background is white.
  • Do not apply outlines to the logo.
  • Do not change the arrangement of the word mark and the graphic symbol.
  • Do not replace the font in the word mark.
  • Do not replace any colors in the logo.

Minimum Size

The minimum height of the horizontal logo must always be 32 pixels for digital medium or 0.75 inches for print. The minimum height of the vertical logo must always be 64 pixels for digital medium and 1.5 inches for print.
