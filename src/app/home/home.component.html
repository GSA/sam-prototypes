Entity Information

You must have an active entity registration in SAM.gov to receive a federal contract or federal assistance award (such as a grant, loan, or other assistance award).

Contracting

Get started doing business with the federal government. The federal government awards contracts to purchase or lease supplies and services (including construction).

Contract Opportunities (fbo.gov)

Contract Opportunities is a government-wide list of notices of proposed contract actions expected to exceed $25,000.

  • Search
  • Run Reports
  • Publish Opportunities (Federal Users)

Contract Data Reports (fpds.gov Reports*)

Contract Data is publicly available information about all unclassified contract actions over the micro-purchase threshold.

  • Run Reports

* FPDS.gov remains the official source for most contract data functionality. All FPDS.gov reports, however, have been transitioned to SAM.gov.

Federal Assistance

Federal assistance programs, services, and activities directly aid organizations, individuals, or state/local/tribal governments. Sectors include education, health, public safety and public welfare - to name a few. Financial assistance is distributed in many forms, including grants, loans, direct payments, and insurance.
