SAM.gov Registration is FREE

There is NO FEE to register your entity or use any of the other functions provided by SAM.gov.

If you receive an email from a company claiming to represent SAM.gov, be cautious. If you get an e-mail from a company offering to help you register in SAM.gov, or asking you to contact them or pay them money, be cautious. These messages are not from the U.S. Federal Government.
