Add a new entity

Your entity will be assigned a unique entity ID when you add it to SAM.gov.

  • Your unique entity ID is permanent. It will be used to identify your entity for as long as it exists as a legal entity.
  • Your entity must have a unique combination of a legal business name and physical address.

Carefully review your existing entities before adding a new one, to prevent duplicates.
