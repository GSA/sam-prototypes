Integrity Information

Integrity information covers the following records used to support award decisions as required by the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) and 2 CFR 200 of the Uniform Guidance. Records include the following.

  • Administrative Agreements
  • Defective Pricing Actions
  • DoD Determinations of Contractor Fault
  • Information on Trafficking in Persons
  • Non-Responsibility Determinations
  • Recipient Not Qualified Determinations
  • Subcontractor Payment Issues
  • Terminations for Default
  • Terminations for Cause
  • Terminations for Material Failure to Comply
