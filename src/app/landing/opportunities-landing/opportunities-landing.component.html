Contract Opportunities

Contract opportunities are procurement notices from federal contracting offices. Anyone interested in doing business with the government can use this system to search opportunities. Opportunities include pre-solicitation notices, solicitation notices, award notices, and sole source notices.

Do I need a user account?

While you don’t need a user account to view contract opportunities, you will need one to access additional capabilities available on SAM.gov. With a user account you can:

  • Save searches
  • Follow changes to opportunities
  • Join interested vendor lists
  • Use the same account to sign in to multiple government websites
