Entity Information

An entity is any person or organization that is registered to do business with the federal government. You must have an active entity registration in SAM to receive a federal contract or federal assistance.

You must be signed in to search for entity registrations

Search Registrations

Enter a Unique Entity ID, CAGE/NCAGE or entity name to start searching. Click Advanced Search to start searching with the advanced filters.

Register your entity to get started doing business with the federal government.
