Entity Information

What is an Entity

Anyone who applies or proposes to receive a federal contracting or assistance award from the federal government must be registered as an entity in SAM.gov. Entities include businesses including small businesses and sole proprieterships; state, local, and tribal governments; universities and hospitals; foreign businesses; and federal agencies who participate in intragovernmental transactions.
